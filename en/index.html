Fantastic Animals

Fox

Foxes are omnivorous mammals belonging to the Canidae family. They are medium-sized canids, characterized by a long snout and a long, bushy tail.

They also have distinctive oval pupils, similar to the vertical pupils of felines.

Of about 40 species recognized as foxes, only 12 belong to the Vulpes genus of "true foxes," of which the red fox is the most common.
